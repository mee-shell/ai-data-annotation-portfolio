# Hallucination Annotation Examples

## Overview

This document provides examples of annotated responses from the hallucination detection dataset.

The examples illustrate how responses are labeled according to the annotation guidelines.

---

## Example 1 – Correct Response

Prompt  
What is the capital of the Netherlands?

AI Response  
Amsterdam is the capital of the Netherlands.

Label  
correct

Explanation  
The information is factually accurate.

---

## Example 2 – Hallucination

Prompt  
Which sea borders the Netherlands?

AI Response  
The Netherlands borders the Baltic Sea.

Label  
hallucination

Explanation  
The Netherlands borders the North Sea, not the Baltic Sea.

---

## Example 3 – Partially Correct

Prompt  
Which river flows through Rotterdam?

AI Response  
Rotterdam lies on the Rhine River.

Label  
partially_correct

Explanation  
Rotterdam lies on the Nieuwe Maas, which is part of the Rhine–Meuse delta.

---

## Example 4 – Hallucinated Statistic

Prompt  
What is the population of the Netherlands?

AI Response  
The Netherlands has a population of about 5 million people.

Label  
hallucination

Explanation  
The population is approximately 17–18 million.

## Example 5 – Correct Cultural Fact

Prompt

## Example 6 – Incorrect Sports Fact

Prompt  
Which Dutch cyclist won the Tour de France five times?

AI Response  
Joop Zoetemelk won the Tour de France five times.

Label  
hallucination

Explanation  
Zoetemelk won the Tour de France once in 1980.
